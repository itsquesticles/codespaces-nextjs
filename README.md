The mistake most athlete websites make is that they are either:

1. A digital museum (great story, no business).
2. A sales page (great business, no soul).
3. AI-generated garbage with generic hero sections and buzzwords.

Mike Metzger is not a normal athlete.

He is one of the most important figures in FMX history. He helped create an entire sport. The website should feel less like an athlete website and more like a modern documentary, archive, gallery, and consulting platform combined.

The business model should be:

> "Access the mind of Mike Metzger."

Not:

> "Book a consultation."

That's a massive difference.
